13 //This is an involved integration test of
14 //haplotype and genotype matrices, and it takes
15 //some time to run.
16 //We simulate a population of N=1000 diploids to equilibrium.
17 //We then construct a haplotype and genotype matrix from a large
18 //sample of diploids. We tests that row and sum columns from the two
19 //matrix types agree. Once those agreement tests pass, we compare the
20 //haplotype matrix to the row/sum columns of a sample based on
21 //KTfwd::sample_separate (fwdpp/sugar/sampling.hpp) for the same diploids.
22 //These last checks ensure that two independent pieces of code, written
23 //at different times, give the same results.
24 //
25 //Then, to really make sure, we do the above tests 1,000 times, evolving
26 //our population 100 generations in between each test.
